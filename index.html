About

Maya Yianni creates a sound of nostalgia and familiarity through her music. Her voice nods towards the late, great jazz singers that inspired her from a young age, but still sounds contemporary, with influences panning from Riot Grrl, Noise, 60's Pop and beyond. Her approach to songwriting is unapologetically auto-biographical and frank, and she writes to magnify both the menial and meaningful moments of her life. Yianni has a DIY approach to her work, which might not be immediately apparent when you listen to it.

These working ways were mainly developed whilst studying at Goldsmiths, London, where Yianni also realised the power of deliberately minimising the creative decisions that appear when creating music. She rarely works with electronics because of this and tends to stick to organic means of songwriting to reduce her distractions. Yianni also sites collaboration with other musicians, artists, and friends, as a crucial part of her artistry. Yianni lives in South East London.
